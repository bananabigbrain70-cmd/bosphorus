# Bosphorus Lounge chatbot sales script

## Simple pitch

Hi, I built a demo AI receptionist for your restaurant.

It can answer common customer questions, collect reservation requests, validate the details, let customers change answers, and send bookings directly to your email.

The main benefit is simple: when someone visits your website or social media after closing hours, they can still request a table instead of leaving or messaging a competitor.

## Demo flow

1. Show the landing page.
2. Click "Reserve a table".
3. Enter a fake name.
4. Enter a phone or email.
5. Enter an invalid date to show validation.
6. Enter a real date.
7. Enter a time.
8. Type "go back" or "change time".
9. Confirm booking.
10. Show the email notification.
